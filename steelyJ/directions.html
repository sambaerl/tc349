Directions

Our launch point is located at Shamrock Park. For more information about the park, visit their website!

From I-94: Take exit 28, follow Old US 31 southeast 11 miles through Berrien Springs and across the St. Joe River. Take the first left, then proceed to make a sharp left turn into the park.

From the South: Take US 31 Bypass to exit 13. Follow Snow Rd east through Berrien Springs and across the St. Joe River. Take the first left, then proceed to make a sharp left turn into the park.
