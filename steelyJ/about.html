Meet Your Captain

Captain Brett Johnson has been fishing the tributaries of the Great Lakes his entire life. A Michigander through and through, Captain Brett was raised in Traverse City, fishing the West and East Bays with a main focus on the Boardman river. Through this, he gained the knowledge and experience needed for the numerous runs of Salmon and Steelhead throughout the season. Captain Brett is a United States Marine Corps active duty Gunnery Sergeant who retired in 2008. He started fishing the St. Joseph River in 1994 while stationed in Southwest Michigan.

"The St. Joseph River system is nothing short of spectacular. The beautiful colors of the fall and the snow white covered banks in the winter with a river loaded with fish. Join me for a day on the River"
